Connecting Parents to Schools Seamlessly

Funza is designed to streamline communication between parents and schools, ensuring you never miss an important update. Stay informed, engaged, and connected to your child's school life with ease.

  • Instant Alerts: Receive real-time notifications about school closures, events, and critical updates.
  • Assignment & Progress Tracker: Stay on top of your child’s homework, assignments, and grades with regular updates.
  • Fee Management: Monitor balances, receive fee reminders, and make payments securely through the app.
